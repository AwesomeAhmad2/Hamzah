What I've Achieved

The following are my CAS reflections starting from September 2018 to March 2020

Creativity

Tuesday, September 11, 2018

Over the summer, i registered in a French course at Alliance Francaise in the Diplomatic Quarter. I would go there to acquire knowledge in the French language and meet other peeople who have different goals than me. One of the reasons i wanted to learn a new language is because i believe in Sapir-Whorf Hypothesis. The hypothesis states that different languages create different ways of thinking. However, there were other reasons why i wanted to learn French.

From this experience, i gained many IB learner profiles. The most notable one was communicator, for the reason that i gained relationships with my classmates and learned the language by communicating in class. After the 7 week course, i felt my perspective of the world changed, which indicates that Sapir-Whorf hypothesis is correct. Aside from the language itself, i learned about the French culture and how they live their daily lives.
